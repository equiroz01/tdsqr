## 4. Description
**Field:** Description (max 4000 characters)

```
TDS QR transforms any TV or display into a dynamic QR code and slideshow presenter. Perfect for businesses, events, restaurants, and retail environments.

HOW IT WORKS

TDS QR uses two modes that work together seamlessly:

TV / Display Mode
Install the app on your TV (Apple TV, iPad, or any device connected to a screen). This device will display your content in beautiful fullscreen presentations.

Control Mode
Use your iPhone as a remote control. Create QR codes, upload images, and manage what appears on the connected display - all wirelessly over your local WiFi network.

KEY FEATURES

- Create custom QR codes with any URL or text
- Upload images from your photo library
- Automatic slideshow presentation
- Secure connection using QR scan or 6-digit PIN
- Works on the same WiFi network
- Dark mode optimized interface
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, Portuguese)

PERFECT FOR

- Restaurants & Cafes: Display menus, WiFi passwords, social media links
- Retail Stores: Show promotions, loyalty programs, product information
- Events & Conferences: Present sponsor content, registration links, schedules
- Offices: Display company information, meeting room QR codes, announcements
- Hotels: Guest information, WiFi access, local recommendations
- Healthcare: Patient check-in, appointment scheduling, information displays

EASY SETUP

1. Open TDS QR on your TV/display device and select TV Mode
2. Open TDS QR on your phone and select Control Mode
3. Scan the QR code on the TV or enter the 6-digit PIN
4. Start adding QR codes and images
5. Press play and your content displays automatically!

No account required. No cloud storage. Your content stays on your devices and transfers directly over your local network for maximum privacy and speed.

Download TDS QR today and turn any screen into a professional digital display!
```

**Explanation:** The description should clearly explain what the app does, its key features, and target audience. Use formatting with headers and bullet points for readability.

## Notes for Submission

1. **Screenshots:** You'll need screenshots for different device sizes (iPhone 6.7", 6.5", 5.5", iPad Pro 12.9"). Show the main screens: mode selection, TV display mode, control mode, QR creation.

2. **App Icon:** Ensure your icon meets Apple's requirements (1024x1024px, no alpha channel, no rounded corners - Apple adds these automatically).

3. **Bundle ID:** `com.equiroz.tdsqr` (already configured)

4. **Encryption:** Your app.json already declares `ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption: false`, which is correct since the app doesn't use encryption beyond standard HTTPS.

5. **Review Notes for Apple:** Consider adding a note explaining the two-device functionality for the App Review team.

**Suggested Review Note:**
```
TDS QR requires two devices to demonstrate full functionality:
1. One device in "TV Mode" to display content
2. Another device in "Control Mode" to manage content

Both devices must be on the same WiFi network. The Control device connects to the TV device by scanning a QR code or entering a 6-digit PIN displayed on the TV screen.

For testing, you can use two iOS devices (or simulators) on the same network.
```
