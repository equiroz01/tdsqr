## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

#### How do I connect two devices?

1. Open TDS QR on your TV/display device and select **TV Mode**
2. Open TDS QR on your phone and select **Control Mode**
3. Either scan the QR code shown on the TV, or tap "Enter PIN manually" and enter the 6-digit PIN
4. Once connected, you can start adding content!

#### Do both devices need to be on the same WiFi?

Yes, both devices must be connected to the same WiFi network. TDS QR uses local network communication, which requires devices to be on the same network.

#### Do I need an internet connection?

No! TDS QR works entirely over your local network. Once the app is installed, no internet connection is required.

#### Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. TDS QR works immediately after installation with no sign-up required.

### Connection Issues

#### I can't connect to the TV device

Try these steps:

1. **Check WiFi:** Ensure both devices are on the same WiFi network
2. **Restart TV Mode:** Close and reopen TV Mode to generate a new PIN
3. **Check PIN:** Make sure you're entering the correct 6-digit PIN
4. **Network settings:** Some networks block device-to-device communication. Try a different network if possible
5. **Firewall:** Enterprise networks may have firewalls blocking local connections

#### The connection keeps dropping

- Move both devices closer to your WiFi router
- Check for WiFi interference from other devices
- Ensure neither device goes to sleep (disable auto-lock temporarily)
- Try restarting both the app and your WiFi router

#### The QR code won't scan

- Ensure adequate lighting on the TV screen
- Hold your phone steady, about 6-12 inches from the screen
- Clean your phone's camera lens
- Try using the manual PIN entry instead

### Content & Display

#### How do I create a QR code?

1. In Control Mode, tap the **QR Codes** tab
2. Enter a name (e.g., "Menu", "WiFi", "Instagram")
3. Enter the URL or text for the QR code
4. Tap **Add QR**
5. Your QR code is now ready to display!

#### How do I add images?

1. In Control Mode, tap the **Images** tab
2. Tap **Select Image**
3. Choose an image from your photo library
4. Optionally add a name/caption
5. The image will be added to your content

#### How do I start the slideshow?

1. Add at least one QR code or image
2. Tap the **Start on TV** button
3. Your content will begin displaying on the TV
4. Use the controls to play/pause or navigate manually

#### Can I change the slideshow timing?

Currently, the slideshow advances automatically at a fixed interval. Custom timing options may be added in future updates.

#### The content isn't showing on the TV

- Ensure the devices are still connected (check for the "Connected" status)
- Try sending a content update from Control Mode
- Restart TV Mode and reconnect

### App Settings

#### How do I change the language?

1. Open TDS QR
2. Tap the **Settings** icon (gear icon)
3. Select **Language**
4. Choose from English, Spanish, or Portuguese

#### How do I delete a QR code or image?

- Swipe left on the item you want to delete
- Tap **Delete** to confirm

### Technical Questions

#### What devices are supported?

TDS QR runs on:
- iPhone (iOS 15.0 or later)
- iPad (iPadOS 15.0 or later)
- Apple TV (tvOS 15.0 or later)

#### Does TDS QR store my data in the cloud?

No. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

#### Is my data secure?

Yes. TDS QR uses secure local connections with PIN verification. Your content never leaves your local network. See our [Security Policy](/security) for details.

#### Does TDS QR use my camera when I'm not scanning?

No. The camera is only active when you're scanning a QR code to connect. It is never used in the background.

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Step-by-Step Diagnostics

If you're experiencing issues, follow these steps:

#### 1. Check Basic Requirements
- [ ] Both devices have TDS QR installed
- [ ] Both devices are on the same WiFi network
- [ ] WiFi is working (test with a website)
- [ ] App has necessary permissions granted

#### 2. Restart the Connection
- [ ] Close TDS QR on both devices completely
- [ ] Open TV Mode first, wait for PIN to appear
- [ ] Open Control Mode and connect

#### 3. Check Permissions
On your iPhone, go to Settings > TDS QR and ensure:
- [ ] Camera: Allowed (or use PIN instead)
- [ ] Photos: Allowed (to add images)
- [ ] Local Network: Allowed (required)

#### 4. Network Troubleshooting
- [ ] Restart your WiFi router
- [ ] Forget and reconnect to WiFi on both devices
- [ ] Try a different WiFi network
- [ ] Check if other devices can communicate on your network

#### 5. App Troubleshooting
- [ ] Force quit and reopen the app
- [ ] Restart your device
- [ ] Check for app updates in the App Store
- [ ] Delete and reinstall the app (note: this will delete saved content)
