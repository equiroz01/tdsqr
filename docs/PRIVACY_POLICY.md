# Privacy Policy

**TDS QR - TV Display Slides + QR**

Last Updated: January 2025

---

## Introduction

TDS QR ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

**Our core principle: Your data stays on your devices. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any external servers.**

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

TDS QR does not collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Browsing history
- Contact information
- Financial information

---

## How the App Works

### Local Data Storage

All data you create within TDS QR is stored locally on your device:

- QR codes you create (names and URLs)
- Images you select for display
- App settings and preferences
- Language preferences

This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

### Local Network Communication

TDS QR enables communication between two devices on the same WiFi network:

- **TV Mode Device:** Receives content to display
- **Control Mode Device:** Sends content to the TV

This communication occurs exclusively over your local network. Data transferred includes:
- QR code information (names and URLs)
- Selected images
- Presentation control commands (play, pause, next, previous)

**No data is transmitted over the internet or to third-party servers.**

---

## Device Permissions

### Camera Access

TDS QR requests camera access for one purpose only:
- To scan QR codes for device pairing

Camera data is:
- Processed locally on your device
- Used only for QR code recognition
- Never stored, recorded, or transmitted

You can deny camera access and use manual PIN entry instead.

### Photo Library Access

TDS QR requests photo library access to:
- Allow you to select images for display presentations

Selected images are:
- Transferred directly to the connected display device
- Sent only over your local WiFi network
- Never uploaded to external servers

### Local Network Access

TDS QR requires local network access to:
- Discover and connect to other TDS QR devices
- Transfer content between paired devices

This permission is essential for the app's core functionality.

---

## Data Security

### Encryption

- Device pairing uses a secure 6-digit PIN verification
- Local network communication uses standard protocols
- No sensitive data is stored in plain text

### Data Retention

- Data exists only while stored on your device
- Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
- No backups are made to external servers

### Your Control

You have complete control over your data:
- Delete QR codes and images at any time
- Disconnect from paired devices instantly
- Remove all data by uninstalling the app
